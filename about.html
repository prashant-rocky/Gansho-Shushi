About Us

The Journey of Ganso Sushi Tradition

At Ganso Sushi, we blend the elegance of Japanese tradition with the freshness of Tokyo’s finest seafood. Located in the heart of Shinjuku, our chefs craft each piece with precision, respect, and passion. Whether you’re savoring classic nigiri, exploring creative rolls, or enjoying seasonal omakase, every bite tells a story of dedication and artistry. Our warm hospitality ensures that every guest feels at home, making your dining experience truly unforgettable.
