What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help remember your preferences, improve site performance, and provide personalized content.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic site functionality, such as navigation and reservations.
  • Performance Cookies: Collect data on how visitors use our site to improve services.
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences, such as language or location.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand website traffic and user behavior.

Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience, personalize menu suggestions, track website performance, and ensure secure reservations.

Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect the functionality of certain features on our website.

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies may be placed by trusted third-party services, such as analytics providers or delivery platforms linked from our site.

Updates to This Policy

We may update our Cookies Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, law, or our services.
